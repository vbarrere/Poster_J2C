\block{Outlook} {
    It is expected that the results obtained for the \textcolor{colorAg}{\textbf{Ag}}-\textcolor{colorCo}{\textbf{Co}} system will show a core-shell configuration, with \textcolor{colorAg}{\textbf{Ag}} atoms preferentially located at the surface of the nanoparticles and \textcolor{colorCo}{\textbf{Co}} atoms in the core.
    Once this results obtained we shown that this method is able to correctly characterize the structure of nanoalloys.
    We will then be able to use this method to characterize more complex systems, such as those composed of three or more elements.
