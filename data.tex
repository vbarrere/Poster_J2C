        \textbf{Distribution of the core-shell index}
        
        \vspace{1cm}
        
        The \textbf{core-shell index} $\eta$ allows to determine the structure of the nanoalloys.
        \begin{equation}
            \eta = 2\left| \frac{n^{out}_{Ag}}{n^{out}_{Ag} + n^{out}_{Co}} - \frac{n_{Ag}}{n_{tot}}\right| + 2\left| \frac{n^{in}_{Ag}}{n^{in}_{Ag} + n^{in}_{Co}} - \frac{n_{Ag}}{n_{tot}}\right| - \frac{d_{com}}{2r_{g}}
        \end{equation}
